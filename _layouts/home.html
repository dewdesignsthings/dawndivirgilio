My Story

Dawn Desirée DiVirgilio is an aspiring artist working in the Chicago and Milwaukee area. Originally from Sarasota, Florida Dawn draws inspiration from her hometown and her life experiences as she made her way to Chicago with only a suitcase and a box of paints. In November of 2005, two of her biggest influences, her mother and grandmother, passed away. She has since focused primarily on her work and coming into her own as an artist and craftsman. Today, Dawn teaches painting and wine classes at The Bottle Shop and is working to create original merchandise with her work.
